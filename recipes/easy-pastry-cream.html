Easy Pastry Cream

Simple to Make Pastry Cream

This pastry cream recipe is adapted from Martha Stewart. It is one of the easiest pastry cream recipes I have every used. It turns out great every time.

Ingredients

  • 1/2 cup sugar
  • 1/3 cup corn starch
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • 6 large egg yolks
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla
  • 2 cups whole milk
  • 2 tablespoons butter

Directions

  1. Whisk everything except the butter in a medium sauce pan. Once everything is mostly combined, add the butter. Bring to boil over medium heat stirring constantly. Let it boil for one minute, still whisking.

  2. Strain the pastry cream through a fine-mesh sieve into a bowl. Place plastic wrap directly on top of the custard making sure to get rid of most of the air pockets. This will keep the custard from getting a hard skin on top.

  3. Refridgerate for at least 2 hours and up to 2 days. Whisk the custard well before using.
